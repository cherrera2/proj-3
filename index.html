About Me

Hello, my name is Cyrus Herrera and I am a student in IIT's ITMD 361 with Professor Stolley. I am a Sophomore studying ITM with a focus on System/IT Administration. I fell in love with the field at a young age. I always loved computers, and as I got older I started to work more and more with them. Eventually I landed a job at my highschool where I repaired and managed a lot of the technology at the school. Apart from technology, I really love music. I make a lot of instrumentals using a program called FL Studio. It's a hobby I have been keeping up with for about a decade now, but I'm hoping that in the future I can make some real money from it.
